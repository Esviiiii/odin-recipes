Pesto Meatball Soup Recipe

  • 12 ounces ground beef
  • 4 ounces ground pork
  • 3 ounces grated Parmesan cheese, or more to taste
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup finley minced onion
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon granulated garlic
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 lemon, zested
